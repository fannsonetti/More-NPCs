Roles

Customers place orders. Dealers work for you and take a cut. Supervisors manage assigned dealers and collect takes. Manager runs business laundering flow.

Standards Meaning

Low through Very High reflect quality expectations and tolerance. Higher standards generally need better product quality to satisfy consistently.

Unlock Progression

Supervisors and manager unlock through progression gates in the mod systems. Use this page to track available roles, cuts, and where each character operates.
